What services does your hospital provide?

Our hospital offers a wide range of services, including general medicine, surgery, cardiology, neurology, pediatrics, gynecology, and diagnostic imaging. We are equipped with state-of-the-art technology to provide comprehensive healthcare.

Are walk-in appointments allowed?

Yes, we accommodate walk-in appointments. However, we recommend booking appointments in advance to minimize wait times and ensure a smooth experience.

Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans. Please contact our billing department for a full list of accepted insurance providers.

How can I book an appointment online?

You can book an appointment through our website by navigating to the "Appointments" section. Fill in your details and select your preferred time slot, and our team will confirm your booking.

What are your visiting hours?

Visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. We encourage visitors to adhere to these hours to ensure the comfort and rest of our patients.

Is emergency care available 24/7?

Yes, our emergency department operates 24/7. Our dedicated team of professionals is always ready to provide immediate medical attention.
